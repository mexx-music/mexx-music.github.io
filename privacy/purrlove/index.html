Privacy Policy – PurrLove

Developer: mexxcatlabstudios

Introduction

PurrLove is a simple Android app that provides relaxing cat purring sounds to help you unwind.

Personal Data

PurrLove does not collect, store, or share personal user data.

Local Audio Playback

All audio plays locally on your device. The app does not require an internet connection for playback.

Analytics and Advertising

PurrLove does not use analytics tools and does not include advertising SDKs.

Children’s Privacy

PurrLove is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact the developer and we will address it.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the developer: mexxcatlabstudios.
